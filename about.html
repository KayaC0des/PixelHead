About Me

Gamer, globetrotter, fitness fiend, food fanatic, music maniac, film binger, martial arts kicker and big time trophy collector.

My Passion for gaming started when I was around five years old. That's when my father brought home our first ever console - SNES Classic. Both of us obsessed about it for over two years, spending hours playing Mario Bros, Contra, MAPPY and other "999 in one" oldie gems. My transformation into gaming geek completed when I was gifted my first CPU at the age of eight!

Games allow me to experience great ideas of what a different world or a different life might look like. When supported by creative narration, a game can deliver an emotional and intelectual entertainment that is vastly different from arts such as Film, Painting, Music or Literature, however it combines together their best qualities.

My favourite games include: The Last Of Us, Hollow Knight, Returnal, Last Guardian, Ori and God of War series.
